Mythopedia: Ancient Wisdom

Your comprehensive guide to Greek gods, monsters, and lore.

Gods & Goddesses

  • Zeus: King of the Gods, God of the Sky, Lightning, and Thunder.
  • Poseidon: God of the Sea, Earthquakes, and Horses.
  • Hades: God of the Underworld and Riches.
  • Athena: Goddess of Wisdom, Warfare, and Handicrafts.
  • Apollo: God of Music, Poetry, Light, Healing, and Archery.
  • Artemis: Goddess of the Hunt, Wilderness, and Childbirth.
  • Ares: God of War.
  • Aphrodite: Goddess of Love, Beauty, Pleasure, and Procreation.
  • Hephaestus: God of Fire, Metallurgy, and Craftsmen.
  • Hermes: Messenger of the Gods, God of Travel, Trade, and Thieves.
  • Dionysus: God of Wine, Festivity, and Ecstasy.
  • Demeter: Goddess of Agriculture and the Harvest.

Monsters & Creatures

  • Minotaur: Half-man, half-bull.
  • Hydra: Multi-headed serpent.
  • Medusa: Gorgon with snake hair, turns to stone.
  • Cyclops: One-eyed giants.
  • Hellhounds: Underworld dogs.

Artifacts & Locations

  • Riptide: Percy's sword, disguised as a pen.
  • Master Bolt: Zeus's lightning bolt.
  • Camp Half-Blood: Training ground for demigods.
  • Mount Olympus: Home of the gods.
  • Underworld: Realm of the dead.
